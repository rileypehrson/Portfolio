Provence, France Travel Brochure

I created this travel brochure concept for tourists. I styled the brochure with a 1950's aesthetic for a nostalgic and fun appeal. I also wanted to practice creating a cohesive theme. The colors and fonts are also inspired by the 1950's. This brochure would be mostly for westerners looking online for a vacation. The brochure would also be available in a printed copy and be sent out by travel companies in partnership with a Provence vacation company.
