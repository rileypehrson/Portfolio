Hawthorne Landscape Gardens

For my Web 2 class we had to build a responsive website in HTML/CSS for a topic of our choice. I wanted to use the color green for a project so I came up with the idea of creating a landscape garden company. I came up with the name Hawthorne Landscape Gardens and logo to reflect their identity of being a high class company. I created a professional, expensive, and sleek design to align with their values.
