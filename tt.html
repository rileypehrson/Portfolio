Treasure Trove

Treasure Trove is a brand concept for a childrens pirate toy company. I wanted to create a logo that would be appealing to children by using bright colors, high contrast, kid-friendly illustrations, and a custom typeface that is clear to read, has subtle texture, and rich with adventurous personality.
